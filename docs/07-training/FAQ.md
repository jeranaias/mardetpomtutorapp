## <a name="getting-started"></a>Getting Started

### Q: How do I access the tutoring system?

**A:** 
1. Open Microsoft Edge browser
2. Go to: https://make.powerapps.com
3. Sign in with your CAC
4. Click "Apps" in left menu
5. Select "MARDET - Language Tutoring System"

Or download the Power Apps mobile app and sign in.

### Q: Do I need special software installed?

**A:** No. The system runs in your browser (Edge recommended) or via the Power Apps mobile app. No installation needed.

### Q: I'm a new student. How do I get started?

**A:** Your account is automatically created when you join the detachment. Just log in with your CAC. Read the Student Quick Start Guide first.

### Q: Can I use my personal computer/phone?

**A:** No. Use government-provided devices only. System requires CAC authentication which doesn't work on personal devices.

### Q: What browsers are supported?

**A:** Microsoft Edge (recommended), Chrome, Firefox. Internet Explorer is NOT supported.

## <a name="booking-scheduling"></a>Booking & Scheduling

### Q: How far in advance can I book appointments?

**A:** Up to 2 weeks (14 days) in advance.

### Q: When can I book appointments?

**A:** 24/7. The system is always available for booking (though tutoring hours are typically 0800-1700 Mon-Fri).

### Q: Can I book multiple appointments at once?

**A:** Yes. Either book them individually, or use the "Recurring Appointment" option to create weekly sessions automatically.

### Q: What if my preferred tutor is fully booked?

**A:** Try:
1. Different time slot
2. Another tutor who teaches your language
3. Check back later - cancellations open slots
4. Contact Tutor Chief if urgent

### Q: Can I request a specific tutor?

**A:** Yes. Select them when booking. But you can work with any tutor who teaches your language.

### Q: What if I need tutoring outside normal hours?

**A:** Contact your Tutor Chief. Special arrangements may be possible for urgent needs or deployment prep.

### Q: How many appointments can I have per week?

**A:** No system limit, but coordinate with your tutor. Most students average 2-3 sessions per week.

### Q: Can I book same-day appointments?

**A:** Yes, if slots are available. But book ahead when possible - popular times fill up.

### Q: What's the maximum session length?

**A:** 120 minutes (2 hours). Most sessions are 60 minutes.

### Q: Can I extend a session if I need more time?

**A:** Only if:
- Your tutor is available
- No one else has that time slot
- Coordinate directly with tutor, not via system

## <a name="cancellations"></a>Cancellations & No-Shows

### Q: How do I cancel an appointment?

**A:** 
1. Go to "My Schedule"
2. Find the appointment
3. Click [Cancel]
4. Confirm cancellation

### Q: How far in advance must I cancel?

**A:** Minimum 24 hours before appointment to avoid a no-show. Earlier is better.

### Q: What if I need to cancel last-minute (<24 hours)?

**A:** You'll be marked as a no-show unless:
- Medical emergency (documentation required)
- Military duty (orders/authorization)
- Pre-approved by Tutor Chief

Contact your tutor directly ASAP.

### Q: What happens if I'm marked no-show?

**A:** 
- 1st no-show: Warning
- 2nd no-show: Counseling
- 3rd no-show: 2-week suspension
- 4th+ no-show: Extended suspension, possible permanent ban

### Q: Can a no-show be reversed?

**A:** Only if:
- You have documentation (medical, military orders)
- It was a system error (provable)
- Tutor confirms you attended but system glitched

Contact admin within 24 hours to dispute.

### Q: What if my tutor cancels on me?

**A:** You'll receive immediate email notification. Rebook at your convenience. This does NOT count against you.

### Q: I was late but still attended. Will I be marked no-show?

**A:** If you arrive within 15 minutes AND notified tutor beforehand, no. If >15 minutes or no notification, possibly yes (tutor's discretion).

### Q: Can I cancel a recurring series?

**A:** Yes. You can cancel:
- Individual appointment in series
- Entire future series (all remaining appointments)

## <a name="session-notes"></a>Session Notes & Documentation

### Q: Who can see my session notes?

**A:** 
- You (the student)
- Your tutor
- Tutor Chiefs
- Administrators
- Your classroom instructors (upon request)

NOT other students.

### Q: When are session notes added?

**A:** Tutors should complete notes within 24 hours of session. You'll receive email when notes are ready.

### Q: What if my tutor doesn't complete session notes?

**A:** Contact the tutor first (reminder). If repeated issue, notify Tutor Chief. Target is 95%+ completion rate.

### Q: Can I add my own notes?

**A:** Not currently. But you can email tutor with additional comments or request edits.

### Q: Are session notes permanent?

**A:** Yes, for duration of your enrollment + 180 days after graduation. Then archived/purged.

### Q: What's included in session notes?

**A:** 
- Materials covered
- Focus areas
- Performance rating
- Homework assigned
- Tutor recommendations
- Challenges observed

### Q: Will bad performance notes affect my grade?

**A:** No. Session notes are separate from classroom grades. They're for tutoring program only, to help you improve.

## <a name="technical-issues"></a>Technical Issues

### Q: The app won't load. What do I do?

**A:** Try in order:
1. Refresh browser (F5)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Close all browser windows, reopen
4. Try different browser (Edge recommended)
5. Restart computer
6. Contact helpdesk if still broken

### Q: I can't log in with my CAC

**A:** Check:
1. CAC inserted correctly
2. CAC reader light is on
3. PIN entered correctly
4. Certificates not expired
5. Using approved browser

Still stuck? Helpdesk: (831) 242-7440

### Q: I get "Access Denied" error

**A:** You may not be in the security group yet. New students added weekly. Contact admin if you've been here >1 week.

### Q: The system is really slow

**A:** 
- Close unused browser tabs
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try off-peak hours (early morning)
- Report to helpdesk if persistent

### Q: My appointment doesn't show in my schedule

**A:** 
- Wait 5 minutes and refresh
- Check "All" appointments view (not just "Scheduled")
- Verify booking confirmation email
- Contact helpdesk if still missing

### Q: I didn't receive confirmation email

**A:** Check:
1. Spam/junk folder
2. @dliflc.edu inbox (not personal email)
3. Email settings allow @dliflc.edu

If appointment is in system but no email, contact helpdesk.

### Q: Can I use the system on my phone?

**A:** Yes! Download "Power Apps" from your app store (government device only). Sign in with CAC.

### Q: Who do I contact for technical problems?

**A:** DCSIT Helpdesk:
- Email: helpdesk@dliflc.edu
- Phone: (831) 242-7440
- Portal: https://dliflc.service-now.com

NOT the Tutor Chief (for tutoring issues only, not technical).

## <a name="policies"></a>Policies & Procedures

### Q: Is tutoring mandatory?

**A:** Not mandatory, but highly recommended. Especially for students below B average or preparing for DLPT.

### Q: Does tutoring count toward class time?

**A:** No. Tutoring is supplemental, outside of classroom hours.

### Q: Can I get tutoring in multiple languages?

**A:** System designed for your primary language program. Multi-language tutoring requires special approval.

### Q: What if I'm switching languages?

**A:** Contact admin to update your profile. Your history in previous language remains for records.

### Q: Is there a limit to how much tutoring I can get?

**A:** No student limit. But tutors have capacity limits. Don't book excessively - be considerate of others.

### Q: Can I tutor other students?

**A:** Only if you're officially designated as a tutor in the system. Contact Tutor Chief if interested.

### Q: What's the privacy policy?

**A:** 
- Your data: Name, rank, email, academic performance
- Access: Controlled by role (students can't see each other)
- Storage: DoD-approved cloud (Microsoft 365)
- Retention: 180 days after graduation, then purged

See full policy: [Link to privacy document]

### Q: Can I see other students' information?

**A:** No. Privacy protected. You only see your own data.

### Q: What data is collected about me?

**A:** 
- Profile: Name, rank, email, CAC EDIPI, language, class
- Appointments: Date, time, tutor, focus areas, status
- Performance: Session notes, performance ratings
- Academic: Grades, DLPT scores (from instructor updates)

NO social security number, home address, or sensitive personal info.

## <a name="for-tutors"></a>For Tutors Specifically

### Q: How do I become a tutor in the system?

**A:** Tutor Chief adds you. Requirements:
- ILR 3/3/2+ in target language
- Teaching experience
- Current military or GS employee
- Complete tutor training

### Q: Can I set my own availability?

**A:** You have a weekly max hours limit. Students book within your available time. To adjust max hours, contact Tutor Chief.

### Q: What if a student books me when I'm not available?

**A:** Shouldn't happen (students only see your available hours). If it does, contact student to reschedule and report bug to helpdesk.

### Q: How do I handle a difficult student?

**A:** 
1. Document specific behaviors in session notes
2. Discuss with student professionally
3. Notify Tutor Chief if issues persist
4. Follow disciplinary procedures

Never handle alone - loop in leadership.

### Q: Can I tutor my own classroom students?

**A:** Yes, unless policy says otherwise. Check with Tutor Chief.

### Q: Do I get credit/compensation for tutoring?

**A:** Depends on your position:
- Military: Additional duty (no extra pay)
- Civilian: May be primary duty or additional (check your PD)
- Contractor: Per contract terms

### Q: What if my session notes are questioned?

**A:** Notes should be:
- Accurate
- Professional
- Specific
- Constructive

If disputed, leadership reviews. Be honest and detailed always.

### Q: How is my performance evaluated?

**A:** Metrics tracked:
- Session notes completion rate (target: 95%+)
- Utilization rate (target: 60-80%)
- Student performance trends
- No-show rate for your appointments
- Feedback from students (informal)

## <a name="for-admins"></a>For Administrators

### Q: How do I add a new student?

**A:** They're auto-added from Azure AD groups. If missing:
1. Verify they're in MARDET_Students group
2. Wait 24 hours for sync
3. Manually add to SharePoint if urgent

### Q: How do I remove a graduated student?

**A:** 
1. Change status to "Graduated" in Students list
2. System hides from active views
3. Data retained 180 days then auto-purged

### Q: Can I modify appointment on behalf of user?

**A:** Yes. SharePoint list ‚Üí Edit item directly. But notify user of change.

### Q: How do I run reports?

**A:** Power BI dashboards have all standard reports. For custom reports, export SharePoint data to Excel.

### Q: What if service account credentials are compromised?

**A:** 
1. Contact DCSIT immediately
2. Disable service account
3. Reset credentials
4. Investigate breach
5. Update all workflows with new credentials

### Q: How do I update system settings?

**A:** Most settings in SharePoint lists or PowerApp edit mode. Major changes require republishing app.

### Q: Where are backups stored?

**A:** Microsoft-managed in SharePoint Online. 93-day retention. Point-in-time restore available.

### Q: Who has admin access?

**A:** 
- Marine Corps Detachment E-9
- Designated admins (2-3 personnel)
- DCSIT (for technical support only)

### Q: How do I transfer ownership?

**A:** Contact DCSIT. They'll:
1. Transfer PowerApp ownership
2. Update workspace permissions
3. Update SharePoint permissions
4. Document change

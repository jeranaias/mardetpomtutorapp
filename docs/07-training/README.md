## Video Tutorial Scripts

### Video 1: Student - Booking Your First Appointment (5 min)

**Script:**

```
[0:00-0:30] INTRO
"Welcome to the MARDET Tutoring System! I'm going to show you how to 
book your first tutoring appointment. It's quick and easy."

[0:30-1:00] LOGIN
"First, open your browser and go to make.powerapps.com. Sign in with
your dliflc.edu account and complete MFA. Click 'Apps' on the left, then select 'MARDET - Language
Tutoring System'."

[1:00-1:30] DASHBOARD
"This is your dashboard. You see your next appointment, total sessions 
this week, and hours this month. To book a new appointment, click 
'Book New Appointment'."

[1:30-2:30] BOOKING FORM
"Select your language - let's say Arabic. Choose your focus areas - 
I'll check Grammar and Vocabulary. Pick your tutor - I'll choose 
SSgt Smith. Select date and time - how about tomorrow at 1400. 
Choose duration - 60 minutes is standard."

[2:30-3:00] LOCATION & NOTES
"Pick your location - I'll do Tutoring Center. Optionally add notes 
about what you want to work on."

[3:00-3:30] CONFLICT CHECK
"See this? Green checkmark means no conflicts. If it's red, choose a 
different time. Everything looks good, so click 'Book Now'."

[3:30-4:00] CONFIRMATION
"Perfect! You'll see a confirmation message and receive an email 
within a minute. The appointment now shows in your schedule."

[4:00-4:30] REMINDERS
"You'll get a reminder email 24 hours before your session. Don't 
forget to show up! If you need to cancel, do it at least 24 hours 
in advance to avoid a no-show."

[4:30-5:00] WRAP-UP
"That's it! You're ready to start getting tutoring. Check your 
progress anytime from the dashboard. Good luck with your language 
studies! Semper Fi!"
```

### Video 2: Tutor - Completing Session Notes (4 min)

**Script:**

```
[0:00-0:30] INTRO
"This tutorial shows tutors how to complete session notes after an 
appointment. Documenting sessions is critical for tracking student 
progress."

[0:30-1:00] ACCESS FORM
"From your dashboard, click 'Session Notes'. You'll see a dropdown 
of completed appointments that need notes. Select the appointment."

[1:00-2:00] REQUIRED FIELDS
"Enter the actual duration in minutes. In the rich text editor, 
document materials covered - be specific about chapters, pages, 
exercises. Check the focus areas you worked on. Rate student 
performance - I'll select 'Good'. Choose participation level - 
'Engaged'."

[2:00-2:45] OPTIONAL FIELDS
"Assign homework - be clear about what the student needs to do. 
Set next session goals. Note any challenges you observed. Add 
recommendations for improvement."

[2:45-3:30] BEST PRACTICES
"Be specific and constructive. Write notes while the session is 
fresh. Target is 95% completion rate within 24 hours. Your notes 
help students improve and provide data for leadership."

[3:30-4:00] WRAP-UP
"Click 'Save Notes' and you're done. The student can now view your 
feedback in their progress tracker. Thanks for your dedication to 
our Marines' education!"
```

FAQ or email mardet_admin@dliflc.edu."

## Support Procedures

### Tier 1: Self-Service

**Students:**
1. Check Student Quick Start Guide
2. Read FAQ
3. Watch video tutorials
4. Try troubleshooting steps

**Expected resolution:** 80% of issues

### Tier 2: Email Support

**Process:**
1. User emails mardet_admin@dliflc.edu
2. Admin responds within 4 business hours
3. Issue resolved via email or scheduled call

**Expected resolution:** 15% of issues

### Tier 3: In-Person Help

**Office Hours:**
- When: Monday-Friday, 1400-1500
- Where: [Admin office location]
- Walk-ins welcome, no appointment needed

**Expected resolution:** 4% of issues

### Tier 4: Technical Escalation

**When:**
- System-wide outage
- Data corruption
- Security concern
- Bug affecting multiple users

**Process:**
1. Admin documents issue thoroughly
2. Opens DCSIT helpdesk ticket
3. Priority: P1 (critical) or P2 (high)
4. Follows up daily until resolved
5. Notifies affected users of status

**Expected:** 1% of issues

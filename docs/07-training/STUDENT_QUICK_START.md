## Common Questions

### Q: How far in advance can I book?

**A:** Up to 2 weeks in advance. Popular times fill up fast, so book early!

### Q: Can I book recurring appointments?

**A:** Yes! When booking, check "Recurring Appointment" and set end date. System creates weekly appointments automatically.

### Q: What if my tutor cancels?

**A:** You'll receive email notification immediately. Rebook with same or different tutor.

### Q: Can I request a specific tutor?

**A:** Yes! Just select them when booking. If unavailable, try different time or choose another tutor.

### Q: How do I know if session was online or in-person?

**A:** Check appointment details. Online sessions include meeting link. In-person shows location (Tutoring Center, Building 637, etc.).

### Q: What if I'm going to be late?

**A:** Email your tutor ASAP (link in confirmation email). If >15 min late without notice, may be marked no-show.

### Q: Can I change an appointment time?

**A:** Cancel current appointment and book new time. Do this 24+ hours in advance to avoid no-show.

### Q: What counts as a no-show?

**A:** 
- Not showing up without canceling
- Canceling <4 hours before session
- Being >15 minutes late without notice

### Q: What happens with 3 no-shows?

**A:** Temporary suspension from tutoring (usually 2 weeks). Meeting with Tutor Chief required before reinstatement.

### Q: Can I see other students' schedules?

**A:** No. You only see your own appointments and progress. Privacy protected.

### Q: How do I access resources?

**A:** Dashboard ‚Üí "Browse Resources" or visit: https://[github-url]/dli-tutoring

### Q: What if I have technical problems?

**A:** Contact DCSIT Helpdesk:
- Email: helpdesk@dliflc.edu
- Phone: (831) 242-7440
- Portal: https://dliflc.service-now.com

### Q: Who do I contact about tutoring (not technical)?

**A:** Your Tutor Chief or mardet_admin@dliflc.edu

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't log in"

**Try:**
1. Use Microsoft Edge (recommended browser)
2. Check CAC is inserted correctly
3. Close all browser windows, reopen
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try incognito/private mode
6. Contact helpdesk if still stuck

### "Conflict detected but time looks open"

**Possible reasons:**
- Tutor has another appointment at that time
- You have overlapping appointment
- System showing cached data

**Fix:** Refresh page or try different time

### "I didn't get confirmation email"

**Check:**
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Correct email in your profile
3. Wait 5 minutes (sometimes delayed)

**Still nothing?** Check "My Schedule" - if appointment is there, it worked. Email might come later.

### "Book button is grayed out"

**Reasons:**
- Required fields not filled
- Conflict detected
- Tutor unavailable at that time
- Past date/time selected

**Fix:** Check all fields, resolve conflict indicator

### "I can't cancel an appointment"

**Reasons:**
- Appointment already started
- Less than 4 hours away (contact tutor directly)
- Already cancelled

**Fix:** Email tutor if urgent
